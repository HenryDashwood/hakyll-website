My Setup

I think it's a good idea to write about how I would hypothetically recreate my configurations for various things. I don't have anything particularly exiting to share. However, even fairly typical configurations are built up over many hours so it's probably worth documenting.

Terminal

We begin on any machine with a terminal. I have never used a Windows machine for programming, or a desktop version of Linux. On MacOS I would go and download iTerm2. I don't actually know, even after several years of using it, what it does that the normal Mac terminal doesn't. It has a prettier icon though which was why I got it.

Inside the terminal for my shell I use ZSH and Oh My ZSH. It has nice autocompletion and the tab button is useful.

Text Editors

I use VSCode for anything substantial and Vim for anything that will only take a few seconds. You can see my settings.json and .vimrc files here.
